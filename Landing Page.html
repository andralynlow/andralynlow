VISIT
YUNNAN
GARDEN

Yunnan Garden is NTU's newly refurbished community garden with something for everyone.
It was first built in the 1950s as part of Nanyang University
and is an iconic landmark of both the Nanyang University of the '60s and NTU today.
Through NTU's revitalisation efforts, the garden has now expanded to cover an area bigger than 12 soccer fields,
and has added 80 new species of plants, different themed gardens, a lake connecting the garden from one end to the other,
and seamless access to NTU's Chinese Heritage Museum.
Come visit the garden and admire its beautiful nature and thundering waterfalls, and appreciate its heritage.
