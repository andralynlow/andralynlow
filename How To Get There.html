Getting to Yunnan Garden



Nearby MRT Stations

Boon Lay (EW27) & Pioneer (EW28)


Bus Services

179 & 179A


Directions

Take Bus 179 or 179A from Boon Lay or Pioneer MRT Station.
The bus will enter NTU.
Get off at the stop named Yunnan Garden.

Note: Bus 179A only runs on weekday mornings.
